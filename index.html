About Us

MODEL SCHOOL DALMIANAGAR , also known as MSD. Model School is One of Bihar's top Educational Institute . Model School marked its humble beginning in 1957. Model School is a Co-Ed school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) . It is managed by Model School Society. Model School offers Intermediate in Science and Commerce.
